SANDRA OH - HISTORY

TIMELINE OF SANDRA OH'S LIFE

  • 1971- Sandra Oh is born
  • 1981- The beginning of her acting career
  • 1990- Her first breakthrough in television
  • 1993- Sandra's college graduation
  • 1994- Sandra moved to Los Angeles
  • 2003-2006- She married, divorced, starred in many films, and won an award
  • 2008- Sandra hosted the Genie Awards
  • 2013- Sandra announced she was leaving Grey's Anatomy
  • 2018- Sandra received an Emmy nomination (first Asian actress)
  • 2019- Won a Governor General's Performing Arts Award

Sandra Oh's Family

Sandra Oh is a Canadian-American actress, who was born on July 20, 1971 in Ontario, Canada. Her parents are South Korean immigrants, Jeon Young-nam, and Oh Jun-su. Oh's siblings include her brother, Ray, and sister, Grace, who all grew up in a christian household.
